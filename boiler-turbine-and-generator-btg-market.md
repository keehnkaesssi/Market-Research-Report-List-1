<p><p>The Boiler, Turbine, and Generator (BTG) market encompasses various segments vital for power generation. Power boilers convert fuel into steam, driving turbines. Gas turbines utilize high-temperature combustion gases for energy, while steam turbines operate using steam produced by boilers. Turbo generators combine turbine and generator functions to produce electricity efficiently. Heat Recovery Steam Generators (HRSG) recover exhaust heat from gas turbines, enhancing efficiency. Together, these components form the backbone of thermal power plants, ensuring reliable energy production.</p></p>
