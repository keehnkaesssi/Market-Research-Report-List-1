<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plow mixers are essential equipment in various industries for achieving uniform mixing of dry bulk materials. They come in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical plow mixers consist of a vertically arranged vessel with plowshares that agitate materials as they rotate, promoting efficient mixing in a compact design. In contrast, horizontal plow mixers have a horizontal drum and offer larger capacities, making them ideal for high-volume applications. Both types ensure homogeneity and are chosen based on material properties and production requirements.</p></p>
