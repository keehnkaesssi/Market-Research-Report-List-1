<p><ul><li>Layered Zirconium Crowns</li><li>Monolithic Zirconium Crowns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconia crowns market is primarily segmented into two types: layered zirconium crowns and monolithic zirconium crowns. Layered zirconium crowns feature a strong zirconia base with a porcelain overlay, providing a natural appearance while maintaining durability. In contrast, monolithic zirconium crowns are made entirely from zirconia, offering superior strength and resistance to wear but a less natural aesthetic. Both types are used in restorative dentistry, catering to different patient needs and preferences regarding aesthetics and longevity.</p></p>
