<p><p>Composite Fabrication Technology encompasses various manufacturing methods, each suited for specific applications. Lay-up involves manually layering materials, while filament winding uses continuous strands for cylindrical products. Injection molding allows complex shapes by injecting resin into molds. Pultrusion pulls fibers through a resin bath for consistent profiles. Compression molding shapes materials by applying heat and pressure. Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) infuses resin into a closed mold. Other methods include advanced techniques like additive manufacturing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
