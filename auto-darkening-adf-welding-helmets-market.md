<p><ul><li>Fixed Shade</li><li>Variable Shade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto Darkening Welding Helmets (ADF) are categorized into two main types: fixed shade and variable shade. Fixed shade helmets offer a single, predetermined level of darkness, providing consistent protection but lacking versatility. Conversely, variable shade helmets enable users to adjust the shade level depending on the welding process, offering enhanced comfort and visibility by automatically adapting to different light conditions. Both types prioritize safety, but variable shade helmets cater to a wider range of welding applications and preferences.</p></p>
