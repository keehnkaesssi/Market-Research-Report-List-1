<p><p>The glass lamination film market comprises three main types: EVA film, PVB film, and other specialized films. EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) film is favored for its excellent optical clarity and adhesion, commonly used in solar panels and architectural glass. PVB (polyvinyl butyral) film is renowned for its durability and safety features, often utilized in automotive and construction applications. Other films include advanced polymers and materials designed for specific applications, catering to diverse industrial needs for enhanced performance and protection.</p></p>
