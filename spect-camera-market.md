<p><strong>The SPECT Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gamma Ray</li><li>X Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SPECT camera market is primarily categorized into gamma ray and X-ray segments. Gamma ray SPECT cameras utilize gamma radiation emitted from radiopharmaceuticals injected into patients, enabling precise imaging of internal organ functions and structures. In contrast, X-ray imaging employs high-energy photons to visualize bones and dense tissues. While both modalities serve crucial roles in diagnostics, gamma ray SPECT is specifically designed for functional imaging, whereas X-ray systems are more focused on structural imaging, addressing distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
