<p><ul><li>Inside Light Source Microscope</li><li>Outside Light Source Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic operating microscope market is primarily categorized into two types: inside light source microscopes and outside light source microscopes. Inside light source microscopes have integrated illumination systems, providing optimal lighting directly from the device, enhancing visibility during delicate eye surgeries. Conversely, outside light source microscopes utilize an external light source, allowing flexibility in positioning and varying light intensity. Both types are essential in ophthalmology, enabling precise, high-magnification visualization for surgical procedures, ultimately improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
