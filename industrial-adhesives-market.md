<p><p>The industrial adhesives market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Water-based Adhesives** utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup.</p><p>2. **Solvent-based Adhesives** contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding but may emit volatile compounds.</p><p>3. **Hot-Melt Adhesives** are thermoplastic materials that bond upon cooling, ideal for quick applications.</p><p>4. **Pressure Sensitive Adhesives** create bond upon contact, commonly used in tapes and labels.</p><p>5. **Others** include specialty adhesives catering to unique industrial needs, such as structural or high-temperature applications.</p></p>
