<p><strong>The HIV Screening Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Immunoassay Test</li><li>Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV screening machines market comprises two main types: Rapid Immunoassay Tests and Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests. Rapid Immunoassay Tests detect antibodies or antigens associated with HIV, providing results in as little as 15-30 minutes, making them user-friendly for clinics and field settings. Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests, on the other hand, identify the virus's genetic material, offering quicker and more accurate results, often within hours. Both types enhance accessibility and expedite the diagnosis of HIV, crucial for effective treatment and prevention.</p></p>
