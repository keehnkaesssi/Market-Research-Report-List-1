<p><p>The polyurethane topcoat market is primarily categorized into two main types: water-based and oil-based topcoats. Water-based topcoats are known for their low VOC emissions, quick drying times, and ease of cleanup with water, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for various applications. In contrast, oil-based topcoats offer superior durability, a rich finish, and excellent adhesion, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and outdoor use, though they have longer drying times and higher VOC content.</p></p>
