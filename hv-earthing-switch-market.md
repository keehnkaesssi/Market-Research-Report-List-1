<p><ul><li>Independent HV Earthing Switches</li><li>Combined HV Earthing Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HV Earthing Switch market comprises two primary types: Independent HV Earthing Switches and Combined HV Earthing Switches. Independent HV Earthing Switches function solely to connect and disconnect the earthing point from the system, ensuring safety during maintenance. In contrast, Combined HV Earthing Switches integrate the earthing function with circuit breaker operations, providing a compact solution that enhances operational efficiency. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety and reliability of high voltage electrical installations.</p></p>
