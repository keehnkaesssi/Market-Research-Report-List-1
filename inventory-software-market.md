<p><p>The inventory software market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise inventory software. Cloud-based inventory software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy access from various devices, making it ideal for businesses that require real-time data and remote management. In contrast, on-premise inventory software is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control and security but often requiring higher initial costs and IT maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
