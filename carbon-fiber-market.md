<p><p>The carbon fiber market can be divided into two primary types: regular-tow carbon fiber and large-tow carbon fiber. Regular-tow carbon fiber, with narrower strands, is commonly used in applications requiring high precision and strength, such as aerospace and automotive industries. Large-tow carbon fiber, on the other hand, features broader strands that improve production efficiency and reduce costs, making it suitable for larger-scale applications like sporting goods and wind turbine blades. Each type serves specific industry needs based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
