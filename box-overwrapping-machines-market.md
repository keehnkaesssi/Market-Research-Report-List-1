<p><p>Box overwrapping machines are categorized into three main types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for wrapping each box, making them suitable for low-volume production. Semi-automatic machines streamline the wrapping process but still require some manual input, balancing efficiency with operator control. Automatic machines fully automate the wrapping process, efficiently handling high volumes with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale operations and improving overall productivity and consistency.</p></p>
