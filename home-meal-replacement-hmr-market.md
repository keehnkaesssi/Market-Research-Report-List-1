<p><p>The Home Meal Replacement (HMR) market includes three primary types: Ready to Cook (RTC), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Eat (RTE). RTC products require minimal preparation, allowing consumers to cook meals at home with fresh ingredients. RTH items are pre-cooked and require reheating, making them convenient for quick meals. RTE options are fully prepared and only need to be served, offering the ultimate convenience for busy consumers seeking quick, satisfying meal solutions without extensive preparation.</p></p>
