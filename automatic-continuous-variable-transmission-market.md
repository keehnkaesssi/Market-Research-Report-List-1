<p><strong>The Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) market can be broadly categorized into two types based on the connection mechanism: belt connection and chain connection. </p><p>Belt-connected CVTs use flexible belts and pulleys to achieve seamless shifting, providing smooth acceleration and fuel efficiency, commonly seen in passenger vehicles. In contrast, chain-connected CVTs employ metal chains for better durability and higher torque handling, making them suitable for heavier applications like trucks and some utility vehicles. Each type caters to distinct performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
