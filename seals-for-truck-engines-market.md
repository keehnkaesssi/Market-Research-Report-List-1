<p><ul><li>Spark Plug Seal</li><li>Nozzle Seal</li><li>Stem Seal</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck engine seals market comprises various types that ensure optimal engine performance and efficiency. Spark plug seals prevent leaks and maintain combustion integrity, while nozzle seals ensure proper fuel delivery by sealing injector connections. Stem seals control oil consumption by sealing the valve stems, preventing oil from entering the combustion chamber. Additionally, other seals encompass a range of components, such as oil pan and crankshaft seals, providing necessary barriers against leaks and contaminants, ultimately extending engine life.</p></p>
