<p><p>AC Current Transformers (CTs) are essential components in electrical metering, allowing the measurement of alternating current in power systems. The two main market types are pin and wire CTs. Pin CTs feature a compact design with pins for easy mounting on circuit boards, making them suitable for rigid applications. Wire CTs, conversely, are designed to encircle conductors and can accommodate larger gauge wires, offering flexibility in installation and making them ideal for versatile and dynamic setups in electrical meters.</p></p>
