Phenolic prepreg offers high thermal resistance, ideal for aerospace and industrial sectors. BMI prepreg provides superior thermal stability and is used in high-performance applications. Cyanate ester prepreg boasts low moisture absorption and high-temperature performance, while polyimide prepreg is recognized for its exceptional thermal and chemical resistance, suited for extreme environments.</p></p>
