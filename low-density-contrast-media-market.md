<p><p>The Low Density Contrast Media market includes primarily two types: iodine-based and barium sulfate compounds. Iodine-based contrast agents are widely used in various imaging techniques like CT scans and X-rays, providing clear visualization of blood vessels and organs. Barium sulfate compounds are specifically utilized for gastrointestinal imaging, enhancing the contrast of the digestive tract during procedures such as barium swallows and enemas. Both types are essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning in medical imaging.</p></p>
