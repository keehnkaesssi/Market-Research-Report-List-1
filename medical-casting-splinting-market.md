<p><p>The medical casting and splinting market comprises two primary types: casting and splinting. Casting is used to immobilize broken bones or severe injuries, typically made from materials like plaster or fiberglass, ensuring stable support during healing. Splinting, on the other hand, provides temporary immobilization for less severe injuries or conditions, allowing for some movement while still offering protection. Both methods are essential in orthopedic treatment, aiding in recovery and promoting proper healing of musculoskeletal injuries.</p></p>
