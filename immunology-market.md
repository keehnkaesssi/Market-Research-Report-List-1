<p><strong>The Immunology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immuno Boosters</li><li>Immunosuppressants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunology market encompasses two main categories: immuno boosters and immunosuppressants. Immuno boosters are products designed to enhance the immune system's response, often used to support overall health and prevent infections. They include vaccines, supplements, and herbal remedies. In contrast, immunosuppressants are medications that decrease immune system activity, commonly prescribed for autoimmune diseases and organ transplantation to prevent rejection. Both market segments address distinct health needs, reflecting the dual role of the immune system in health and disease management.</p></p>
