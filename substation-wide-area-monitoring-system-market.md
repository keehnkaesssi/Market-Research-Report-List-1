<p><p>The Substation Wide Area Monitoring System market encompasses several key components. Phasor Measurement Units (PMUs) capture real-time electrical data from substations, providing crucial insights into grid stability. Phasor Data Concentrators (PDCs) aggregate data from multiple PMUs for analysis and decision-making. Communication networks are essential for transmitting this data efficiently, ensuring robust connectivity. PDC solutions encompass software and hardware that facilitate data processing and visualization, enhancing overall monitoring capabilities and enabling better control of power systems across wide areas.</p></p>
