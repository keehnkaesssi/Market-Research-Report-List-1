<p><p>The metal cutting tools market comprises several types, including cemented carbide, high-speed steel, ceramics, diamond, and others. Cemented carbide tools offer excellent hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications. High-speed steel tools provide good toughness and are often used for general machining. Ceramics are favored for their high-temperature resistance and are ideal for hardened materials. Diamond tools, known for their exceptional cutting performance, are used for precision applications.
