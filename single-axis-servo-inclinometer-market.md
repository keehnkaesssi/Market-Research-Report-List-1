<p><ul><li>Digital Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-axis servo-inclinometer market is categorized into digital output and analog output types. Digital output inclinometers provide precise digital signals representing tilt angles, offering enhanced accuracy and easy integration with modern digital systems. In contrast, analog output inclinometers deliver continuous voltage or current signals proportional to the tilt angle, facilitating compatibility with traditional analog measurement equipment. Both types serve essential roles in applications such as construction, aerospace, and industrial automation, ensuring accurate tilt measurement for effective monitoring and control.</p></p>
