<p><p>The silicone-coated release liner market is categorized into one-sided and double-sided silicone types. One-sided silicone release liners feature a silicone coating on one side, providing efficient release properties for adhesive applications like labels and tapes. In contrast, double-sided silicone release liners have silicone coatings on both sides, enabling the use of adhesives on both surfaces, ideal for applications requiring bonding on both sides, such as in packaging and laminate solutions. Both types enhance product performance and versatility in various industries.</p></p>
