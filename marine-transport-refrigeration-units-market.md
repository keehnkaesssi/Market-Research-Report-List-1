<p><strong>The Marine Transport Refrigeration Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open</li><li>Semi-Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine transport refrigeration units are categorized into open and semi-closed markets based on their operational mechanisms. Open market units employ a direct cooling system using ambient air, making them suitable for various marine vessels. In contrast, semi-closed market units feature a more advanced cooling system that recirculates coolant, allowing for better temperature control and energy efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in preserving perishable cargo during sea transport, ensuring quality and safety throughout the shipping process.</p></p>
