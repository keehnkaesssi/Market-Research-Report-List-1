<p><p>The aesthetic fillers market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable fillers. Absorbable fillers, typically made from natural substances like hyaluronic acid, provide temporary results, usually lasting several months to a year, and are popular for facial volume and wrinkle reduction. In contrast, non-absorbable fillers, such as silicone or polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA), offer permanent enhancements but carry longer-term risks and complications. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for aesthetic improvements and longevity of results.</p></p>
