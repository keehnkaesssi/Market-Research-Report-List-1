<p><p>The heavy rotary drilling rig market can be categorized into two main types: crawler and rubber-tired rigs. Crawler rigs are equipped with a tracked undercarriage, offering superior stability and mobility on uneven terrain, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in construction and oil drilling. In contrast, rubber-tired rigs utilize wheels for movement, providing faster transit speeds and greater maneuverability on paved surfaces. Each type serves specific operational needs, balancing stability, mobility, and application versatility within various drilling environments.</p></p>
