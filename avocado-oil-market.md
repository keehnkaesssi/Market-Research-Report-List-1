<p><ul><li>Refined Avocado Oil</li><li>Extra Virgin Avocado Oil</li><li>Crude Avocado Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The avocado oil market includes three primary types: refined, extra virgin, and crude. Refined avocado oil undergoes processing to remove impurities and enhance its smoke point, making it suitable for high-heat cooking. Extra virgin avocado oil is cold-pressed from ripe avocados, retaining a rich flavor and nutrients, ideal for dressings and dips. Crude avocado oil is minimally processed and retains a stronger avocado taste, often used in cosmetics and personal care products. Each type serves distinct culinary and industrial applications.</p></p>
