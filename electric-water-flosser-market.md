<p><ul><li>Constant Frequency</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric water flosser market can be categorized into two main types: constant frequency and frequency conversion. Constant frequency water flossers operate at a fixed pulse rate, providing consistent pressure for effective plaque removal. In contrast, frequency conversion models offer adjustable pulsation rates, allowing users to customize their flossing experience based on personal preferences and sensitivity. This variability enhances user comfort and effectiveness, catering to a broader audience seeking tailored oral care solutions.</p></p>
