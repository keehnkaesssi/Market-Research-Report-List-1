<p><strong>The Parallel Twin Screw Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Co-rotating</li><li>Counter-rotating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parallel twin screw extruder market is mainly divided into two types: co-rotating and counter-rotating extruders. Co-rotating extruders feature screws that rotate in the same direction, offering efficient mixing and flexibility for processing a wide range of materials. They enhance melt homogeneity and material conveying. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, providing excellent shear and high-viscosity material processing. This design is often used for specific applications requiring controlled melting and degassing.</p></p>
