<p><strong>The Hydraulic Displacement Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Piston Pumps</li><li>Radial Piston Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic displacement pump market comprises primarily two types: axial piston pumps and radial piston pumps. Axial piston pumps feature pistons aligned parallel to the drive shaft, providing high efficiency and compact design, making them suitable for variable flow applications. In contrast, radial piston pumps have pistons arranged radially around a central drive shaft, offering high pressure capabilities and durability, ideal for heavy machinery and industrial applications. Both types are crucial in various sectors, including construction, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
