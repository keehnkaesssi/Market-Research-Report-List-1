<p><p>The Oral Clinical Nutritional Supplements market comprises three main types: Liquid, Semi-Solid, and Powder supplements. Liquid supplements are easily digestible and often offer instant energy, making them ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties. Semi-solid supplements are textured formulations that provide nutritional value while being easier to consume than solids, beneficial for individuals with varying dietary needs. Powder supplements are versatile, allowing for customizability in intake and dosage, and can be mixed with liquids or foods, catering to a broader range of patients.</p></p>
