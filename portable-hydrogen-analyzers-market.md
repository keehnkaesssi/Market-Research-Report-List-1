<p><p>The portable hydrogen analyzers market primarily includes thermal conductivity hydrogen analyzers and explosion-proof hydrogen analyzers. Thermal conductivity analyzers measure hydrogen concentration based on the thermal conductivity differences between hydrogen and other gases, offering precise readings in various environments. In contrast, explosion-proof hydrogen analyzers are designed with safety features to operate in hazardous environments where flammable gases may be present. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as chemical processing, petrochemicals, and environmental monitoring, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
